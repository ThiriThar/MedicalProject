Founder of HERA International Hospital

Mr. William Tribble [Founder & CEO]

William is a doctor himself who started to build HERA International Hospital in 1998 with the help of the government. His proposal to the government was successful as his aims and objectives for this hospital were to provide best and high quality services for everyone.

He began the project in early 1992 along with his co-workers from his previous hospital where he worked as General Surgeon.

In 2024, HERA Hospital is one of the largest and most popular hospitals in UK. Everyone meets their satisfaction in here.
